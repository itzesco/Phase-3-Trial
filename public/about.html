About Us

Project Overview

Lab Reservation System is a web-based application built specifically for De La Salle University students and lab technicians. The system streamlines the process of reserving computer lab slots across five different laboratories, allowing users to check real-time availability, manage schedules, and personalize their profiles. Whether you're a student needing a quiet seat to study or a lab technician managing daily bookings, this platform ensures a seamless and secure experience. With features like reservation anonymity, technician tools, session management, and user profiles, the system brings convenience and structure to lab usage on campus. By integrating modern web technologies, the project aims to reduce booking conflicts, improve lab utilization, and enhance user transparency through intuitive design and functionality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students and lab technicians at De La Salle University with a seamless, reliable, and user-friendly platform for managing computer lab reservations. We aim to enhance productivity, reduce scheduling conflicts, and promote efficient use of campus resources through technology-driven solutions. By prioritizing accessibility, security, and user experience, we strive to support a more organized and tech-savvy academic environment.
